project description

Nearly all production processes use convenient machinery and sometimes even AI, but rarely in the creative designing process. At least, that was the case when I started this project back in early 2022. At the start of this project, almost no one had heard about the existence of Artificial Intelligence. It may be hard to imagine, but it was an incredibly difficult process to generate images based on a short textual description (prompt) back then. Now, many companies such as OpenAI (ChatGPT) and Google offer services to use AI to create images in a matter of seconds. When I presented the idea of using AI in the creative process in early 2022, it was seen as a 'revolutionary concept,' praised by both teachers and fellow students. However, when the project came to an end, developments in the field of Artificial Intelligence started increasing rapidly. So much so that many people, such as graphic designers and artists, started worrying about the future of their profession and started forming strong opinions.
