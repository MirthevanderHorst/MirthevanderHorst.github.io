Project Overview

This dining table was crafted using reclaimed wood, giving it a rustic yet modern feel. The design was inspired by natural elements, and it was custom-made to fit the client's dining room perfectly.

Materials Used

  • Reclaimed Wood (Oak)
  • Steel Legs (Powder-Coated)
  • Polyurethane Finish

Process

The process involved carefully sourcing the reclaimed wood, sanding it to a smooth finish, and applying a durable polyurethane coating. The steel legs were custom-made and powder-coated to resist wear and tear.
