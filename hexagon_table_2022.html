Hexagon Boardgame Table

Get together and have some fun! Here you can see a few pictures of one of my most challenging projects so far. A coffee table that encourages people to sit together and really get to know each other! You can easily store small games or snacks in the middle box, or take it out all together for a lighter visual. It's all your choice! The hexagonal shape forces bigger groups of players closer together (no more people on the far end of the table!) whilst still having straight edges as reference for your game pieces. Another bonus: the drawer underneath is big enough to comfortably store magazines. Definitely a challenge and brain-breaker to make all those corners and edges, but that's what makes woodworking so much fun!

Materials used

  • Solid Ash
  • Birch Plywood (for the drawer)
  • Wood Glue
  • Domino Tenons
  • Ball Bearing Slides
  • Osmo Hardwax Oil
